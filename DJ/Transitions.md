# Transitions

### Back spin
1. Engage Vinyl Mode: Ensure the jog wheel or platter on your DJ controller is active so it registers a physical touch.

2. Time the Phrase: Wait for the end of a musical phrase (typically just before a drop or a new vocal begins).

3. Whip the Platter: Firmly spin the jog wheel counter-clockwise (backward) with the palm of your hand to create a rewind sound.

4. Trigger the Next Track: Immediately drop the volume fader on the outgoing track while slamming in your new track on the downbeat.

Add effects like echo or reverb.
