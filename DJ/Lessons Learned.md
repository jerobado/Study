# Lessons Learned

Organize your songs in DJ sets that have the following:
- intro (warmp-up)
- build-up
- peak (climax)
- outro (cooldown)

Use Harmonic mixing to identify songs that complement each other when transitioning.

Don't forget to **key lock** when beatmatching.

You can slowly adjust the current song's BPM to its original BPM after beat matching.

In DJ mixing, adjusting the master volume knob doesn't remove clipping, you need to adjust either the gain or the EQ knobs.

Level of your next song should match the currently playing song. Adjust the gain knob to make it match.
